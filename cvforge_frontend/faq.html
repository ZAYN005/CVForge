Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered. Quick, clear and professional.

What is CVForge?

CVForge is a modern resume builder that offers ready-made templates and smart tools to create a professional resume in minutes.

Can I customize fonts, colors, and layout?

Yes, you can fully customize fonts, colors, spacing and section layout to match your branding.

Can I download my resume as PDF?

Yes, CVForge allows high-quality PDF downloads optimized for ATS and HR readability.

Is CVForge mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. CVForge is fully responsive and works smoothly on mobile devices.

Is my data safe?

Your data is securely stored using encrypted sessions and high-level security practices.

Will my resume save automatically?

Yes! Your progress is auto-saved, so you never lose your work.

Can I create multiple resume versions?

Yes, you can build and manage multiple resume versions for different job applications.

Can I upload my old resume to autofill?

Yes! CVForge supports resume parsing to automatically extract information from your old resume.

How long does it take to build a resume?

With our guided builder, you can complete a professional resume in under 5 minutes.

Is CVForge available globally?

Yes! Users from any country can create and download resumes easily using CVForge.
